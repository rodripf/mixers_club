# Privacy Policy — Mixers Club

_Last updated: June 6, 2026_

---

## 1. Who We Are

Mixers Club is an independent browser extension that adds community reviews and trending recipes to the Cookidoo platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in any way related to Thermomix, Vorwerk, or any of their subsidiaries or partners.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary for the extension to function:

- **Username** — the display name you choose when you sign up. Stored in our database.
- **Email hash** — a one-way cryptographic hash (SHA-256) of your email address, used solely to display your Gravatar avatar. We **never** store your plain email address. The hash cannot be reversed to recover your email.
- **Reviews** — the text, star rating, and type you submit. These are public and attributed to your username.
- **Votes** — your like or dislike on each review. Stored anonymously against your user ID.

---

## 3. What We Do Not Collect

- We do **not** collect your plain email address at any point after authentication.
- We do **not** collect any browsing history, page content, or activity on Cookidoo beyond what you explicitly submit through the extension.
- We do **not** collect any telemetry, analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics.
- We do **not** use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party tracking technology.

---

## 4. Authentication

Sign-in is handled via magic link (passwordless email). Your email is used by our authentication partner to send you the sign-in link and to maintain your session. We do not receive or store the plain email — only the hash described above is retained in our database after the initial authentication step.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

### Supabase

We use [Supabase](https://supabase.com) as our backend and authentication provider. Your authentication data (email address, session tokens) is handled by Supabase under their own [Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy). We have no control over Supabase's data handling practices.

### Gravatar

Avatar images are loaded from [Gravatar](https://gravatar.com) using the SHA-256 hash of your email address. Gravatar is an independent service operated by Automattic. Gravatar may use this hash in accordance with their own privacy policy.

### Google Translate (optional)

The "Translate" button uses an unofficial Google Translate endpoint. If you click it, the review text is sent to Google's servers for translation. This feature is opt-in and only triggered by your explicit action. We have no control over how Google handles this data.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account and all associated data (username, email hash, reviews, votes) remain stored until you request deletion. To request deletion of your account and data, contact us at the address below.

## 8. User-Generated Content

All reviews, ratings, and comments published through Mixers Club are the sole responsibility of their authors. Mixers Club does not verify, endorse, or take responsibility for any advice, recipe modification, or statement posted by users.

**Use any advice or recipe modifications at your own risk.** Cooking modifications may affect food safety, allergen content, and nutritional value. Always apply your own judgment before following community suggestions.

---

## 9. Community Moderation

Mixers Club is a self-moderated community. Users can flag low-quality or harmful content by using the like and dislike buttons. Reviews with a significantly negative vote ratio are automatically hidden.

The following content is **strictly prohibited**:

- Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic
- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks against any individual or group
- Content promoting or glorifying illegal activities
- Spam, advertising, or promotional content unrelated to recipes
- Misinformation that could endanger health or food safety
- Impersonation of other users or public figures
- Content that violates applicable laws

Mixers Club provides the tools for the community to moderate itself. We are not responsible for individual posts that have not yet been voted down, and we do not guarantee the removal of any specific content. If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, use the dislike button to contribute to the community moderation process.
